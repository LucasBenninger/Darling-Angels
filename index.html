About Me

My name is Angel Michielsen.
I am a photographer new to the scene. However, I have been taking photos since I got my first 35mm camera in the 10th grade.

Photography is my life's passion. Every shoot is a new opportunity to learn and grow.

Services

What I Do.

I have photography experience in the following; children, lifestyle photos, couples, maternity photos, weddings and small gatherings, family's and more.

I also can provide your photos in a few different varieties, all of which can be decided by you

Photo Books

A collection of your photos into a hard or soft cover photo book.

Framed or Canvas Pictures

Printed versions of your favourite photos in a picture frame, or printed onto a canvas.

Electronic Copies

An electronic copy of your photos on a CD or USB drive.

Printed photos

Your photos, in the simplest way. Printed onto high quality photo paper for you to frame yourself.
