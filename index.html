Services

What I Do.

I have photography experience in the following; children, lifestyle photos,
couples, maternity photos, weddings and small gatherings, family's, charity
events and more.

I also can provide your photos in a few different varieties, all of which can
be decided by you.

  • Photo BooksA collection of your photos into a hard or soft cover photo book.
  • Electronic CopiesAn electronic copy of your photos on a CD or USB drive.
  • Framed or Canvas PicturesPrinted versions of your favourite photos in a picture frame, or printed onto a canvas.
  • Printed PhotosYour photos, in the simplest way. Printed onto high quality photo paper for you to frame yourself.
